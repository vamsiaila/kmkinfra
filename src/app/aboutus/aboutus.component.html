Began 1992 as a contract company for the government, KM Kohinoor Infra is now a reputed name in real estate construction segment. Driven by the fervent desire to offer world class development projects, the company since 2004 has been into building independent villas, duplex villas as well as apartments. It has associated with well-known construction companies such as RR Group of Companies, Baycon Builders and Aftech amongst others. While some projects are Hyderabad Metropolitan Development Authority (HMDA) approved, some are registered under gram panchayat layout.

Few of the projects include ‘Pariwar Town’ developed across 14 acres. KM Kohinoor Infra currently selling one of the best HMDA Approved Layout of Hyderabad i.e., “Kohinoor County” comprising open plots and independent villas on a land area of 27 acres. The company is also working with Baycon Builders to build independent villas on 340 acres land near Shankarpally area in Hyderabad. The Kohinoor Gardenia Luxury Villa Compound Project of 100 acres will comprise of 800 villas. KM Kohinoor Infra strives to ensure that the each of the projects offer spacious and modern designs combined with ample greenery.

Adhering to the property guidelines and regulations helps it offer consumers hassle-free home purchasing and ownership. Ultimate client satisfaction and value for money is the main aim of the company. Each of the team members at KM Kohinoor Infra pride themselves to staying true and committed to the ideas of offering quality work while maintaining ethical business standards.
